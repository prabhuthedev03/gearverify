The Sovereign Validation Initiative

In an era of cloud-dependent diagnostics and invasive telemetry, GearVerify stands as a fortress of local-first validation. We believe that hardware owners deserve to know exactly what their silicon is capable of—without uploading their data to a remote server.

Our Technology: WebGPU Native

GearVerify is built on the bleeding edge of browser technology. Unlike legacy tools that rely on WebGL rasterization hacks, our diagnostics utilize WebGPU Compute Shaders (WGSL). This allows us to access the raw parallel processing power of your GPU—bypassing high-level OS driver abstractions—to deliver synthetic benchmark results that rival native desktop applications.

Commitment to Data Privacy

We operate under a strict "Sovereign Data Protocol". This means:

  • ✅ Local Execution: 100% of diagnostic code runs in your local V8 Isolate.
  • ✅ Zero Telemetry: Your hardware specifications, serial numbers, and benchmark scores are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly submit a validation report.
  • ✅ Ephemeral State: Once you close the tab, all memory buffers are cleared. Nothing remains.
