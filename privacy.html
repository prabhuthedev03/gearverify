Sovereign Data Protocol

1. Local-First Architecture
GearVerify operates under a strict "Local-First" architecture. Our hardware validation tools (CPU Stress, GPU Benchmark, WebCam Testing, etc.) execute entirely within your browser's sandboxed environment via JavaScript, WebAssembly, and WebGPU. No hardware telemetry, video feeds, or microphone audio are ever uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Minimization
We minimize data collection to the absolute essentials required for site operation:

  • Technical Cookies: Used solely for maintaining session state (e.g., active theme preference).
  • Analytics: We use privacy-preserving analytics to count visitors and page views. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored.

3. Third-Party Partners
We partner with trusted entities to support the laboratory:

  • Google AdSense: Vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Amazon Associates: As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases via our "Laboratory Recommendation" links. This does not affect the purchase price for you.

4. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
Since we do not collect personal data, account information, or persistent tracking profiles, there is generally no data to request or delete. However, you maintain full control over your browser's local storage and cookie settings.
